对AI builder而言，这项研究敲响了一个重要警钟：仅关注模型准确率（accuracy）是不够的，还需要衡量AI系统对用户行为与决策质量的实际影响。从产品设计角度看，过度顺畅的AI建议可能导致用户"自动驾驶"式依赖，削弱批判性思维。算法侧需要思考如何设计不确定性表达（如置信度可视化、主动质疑用户），产品侧则应考虑交互模式——例如让AI先输出推理过程再给结论，或设置"建议-用户判断-反馈"的闭环。这本质上是一个HCI（人机交互）层面的优化问题，也是区分"好用"和"有用"的关键。
For AI builders, this study is an important wake-up call: measuring model accuracy alone is insufficient — we also need to assess how AI systems actually impact user behavior and decision quality. From a product design perspective, overly smooth AI suggestions can lead to "autopilot" dependency, weakening critical thinking. On the algorithm side, we need to design better uncertainty expression (e.g., confidence visualization, proactive questioning). On the product side, interaction patterns should be reconsidered — such as having AI output reasoning before conclusions, or building a "suggestion → user judgment → feedback" loop. This is fundamentally an HCI optimization problem, and the key differentiator between "feels good to use" and "actually improves outcomes."
