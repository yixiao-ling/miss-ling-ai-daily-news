// 影响分析// Impact Analysis
对AI builder而言，该讨论的核心启示是：1）选择"开放权重"模型（如Llama、Mistral）可获得较好的产品性能，但需警惕许可证限制（如商业使用条款、衍生模型约束）；2）真正的开源AI要求公开训练数据和完整训练流程，这在当前几乎所有主流模型中都未做到，意味着算法层面的可复现性和透明度仍是短板；3）从产品角度看，依赖非真开源模型存在供应链风险——当上游模型变更许可证或条款时，下游产品可能被迫调整。建议团队在技术选型时，既要评估模型的benchmark表现，也要将许可证条款和供应链稳定性纳入考量。
For AI builders, the key takeaways are: 1) Choosing "open-weight" models (e.g., Llama, Mistral) offers strong product performance but requires vigilance on license restrictions (commercial use terms, derivative model constraints); 2) True open source AI demands full training data and process disclosure, which none of the current mainstream models fully achieve—meaning algorithmic reproducibility remains a weakness; 3) From a product perspective, relying on non-true-open-source models creates supply chain risk: when upstream model licenses change, downstream products may be forced to adapt. Teams should evaluate not just benchmark performance but also license terms and supply chain stability during model selection.
