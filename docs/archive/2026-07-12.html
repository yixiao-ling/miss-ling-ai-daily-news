一种名为 Ghost Font（幽灵字体）的新字体被设计出来，人类肉眼可以正常阅读，但 OCR（光学字符识别）和 AI 视觉模型无法识别其字符。其原理是通过对字形进行微妙的几何扭曲，破坏 AI 模型的特征提取模式，同时保留人眼的可读性。这暴露了当前视觉 AI 与人类视觉系统之间的根本差异。 A new typeface called "Ghost Font" has been designed to be perfectly readable by humans while remaining completely unrecognizable to OCR systems and AI vision models. It achieves this through subtle geometric distortions of glyph shapes that break the feature extraction patterns of AI models, while preserving human readability. This exposes a fundamental gap between current visual AI and human visual perception.

对 AI builder 而言，Ghost Font 具有多重警示意义。技术层面：它揭示了当前主流 OCR 和视觉 Transformer 模型对字符特征的建模方式存在致命盲区——模型依赖特定尺度的局部纹理与轮廓规律，而人类视觉依赖整体形状认知。这意味着即使是最先进的视觉模型（如 CLIP、GPT-4V）在面对设计好的对抗样本时仍然脆弱。产品层面：这类字体可被用于绕过内容审核系统（如验证码破解、敏感文本隐藏），也可能催生新的反爬虫与数据保护方案。同时，它也反向启示我们——或许可以在训练中引入此类对抗性字体数据来提升模型的鲁棒性。商业层面：对依赖 OCR 的业务（文档识别、发票解析、车牌识别）意味着需要评估当前模型对这种字形变体的脆弱性。
For AI builders, Ghost Font carries multiple significant implications. Technically, it reveals a critical blind spot in how current OCR and vision Transformer models model character features — they rely on localized texture and contour patterns at specific scales, while humans rely on holistic shape recognition. This means even the most advanced vision models (CLIP, GPT-4V) remain vulnerable to well-designed adversarial examples. On the product side, such fonts could be used to bypass content moderation systems (CAPTCHA cracking, hidden sensitive text) and may also inspire new anti-scraping and data protection solutions. Conversely, it suggests we could introduce such adversarial font data into training to improve model robustness. Commercially, businesses relying on OCR (document recognition, invoice parsing, license plate recognition) need to assess their models' vulnerability to such glyph variations.
